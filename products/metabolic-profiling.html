Metabolic Profiling

Why is strategy not becoming performance?

What is the metabolic condition of this system right now?

Not how good the strategy deck looks. Not how ambitious the OKRs read.

Metabolic Profiling measures how effectively the organization converts signal into outcomes under the conditions it currently faces.

It is the structural truth of the organism as it exists today.

What It Measures

Five metabolic pulse dimensions that determine whether performance compounds or decays.

Density

Volume of signals relative to cognitive and structural bandwidth.

Velocity

Time lag from pattern recognition to committed action.

Accuracy

Error rate in interpretation and routing across the decision topology.

Vitality

Ability to adapt under stress, change, or load spikes without losing direction.

Marginal Cost

Coordination overhead required per additional decision.

These are leading indicators of future performance, not opinions.
