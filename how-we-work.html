04. AI Usage Policy

Our AI usage is assistive, not determinative.

We use AI for:

Structural summarisation of long corpora

Pattern highlighting

Metadata tagging and document clustering

Hypothesis checking acceleration

We do not use AI for:

Final metabolic scoring

Outcome probability determination

Archetype classification

Failure window projection

Those remain model-based, not generative.

When we use LLMs for triage, we employ blind scoring by three separate models at temperature zero. Consensus determines inclusion. Disagreement triggers manual review.

AI helps process noise faster. It does not make the decision.
