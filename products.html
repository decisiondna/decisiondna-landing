Privacy & Security

What happens to your data

The diagnostic is designed around a single principle: your documents never leave your machine.

What stays on your machine

All document files

PDF, DOCX, PPTX — every source file remains on your workstation. The plugin reads them locally.

Full text extraction

Document content is parsed and processed entirely client-side. No text is transmitted.

Decision episode identification

The plugin identifies and structures decision episodes from your documents locally.

What is transmitted for scoring

Anonymized signal vectors

Numerical feature vectors — the six dimension scores as numbers. No text, no context, no identifiable content.

Decision type counts

Aggregate counts — how many decisions of each type and their status distributions. No narrative content.

Rhetorical metrics

Statistical measures of language patterns. Not the language itself.

GDPR Compliance

No personal data transmitted. Anonymized payloads retained 90 days, then auto-purged. Immediate deletion available via diagnostic UUID (Article 17).

EU Data Residency

Scoring infrastructure on GCP europe-west4 (Netherlands). Data does not leave EU jurisdiction.

Authentication

API gated through Cloudflare Access. Authenticated access, identity-blind payloads. No document reconstruction possible from transmitted data.
