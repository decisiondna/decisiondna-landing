What We Measure

Six dimensions of decision-making

Every organization has a metabolic signature — a measurable pattern in how it makes, executes, and sustains decisions. DecisionDNA reads that signature.

Density

How many meaningful decisions the system produces relative to its capacity.

Velocity

How long it takes to move from recognition to committed action.

Vitality

How long decisions stick — and whether the system can renew them.

Marginal Cost

The resource cost of producing one additional decision.

Selection

How accurately the organization chooses the right problems to solve.

Execution

How well chosen actions are implemented and sustained.
